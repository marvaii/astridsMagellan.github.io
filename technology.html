Tech Observation: In major foot traffic areas, there are indicators on the ground that light up, which indicate to you if it is safe to cross. This is due to the long wait between red and green lights. The intersection closest to my accommodation stops all traffic because you can cross diagonally.

Urbanization Observation: The T-money card ( public transport card) is available at most convenience stores; this card works as a debit card and a public transport card. You can reload money in person at most convenience stores or at the reloading kiosk found at most metro stations.

Tech Observation: On the metro and bus, there is a speaker that alerts you of the upcoming stop in Korean, Japanese, and English.

Urbanization Observation: On the metro, some boundaries separate the metro rails and people. They do not open until the train has fully stopped.

Urbanization Observation: Some crosswalks have giant “beach” umbrellas to protect you from the violent UV rays from the sun.

Urbanization Observation: All public signs are in Korean and English and are color-coded so that you can associate colors with destinations. For example, all exit signs at the metro station are yellow.

Urbanization Observation: On every bus and metro there is priority seating for the elderly and expectant mothers. These seats are rarely sat bystanders even if one is there or if the the ride is crowded.

Tech Observation:In Hongdae, there were air-conditioned bus stations.

Urbanization Observation: To get to Gangnam, you had to cross the Han River. I think it goes underground because its very dark until you reach the other side.

Urbanization Observation:All waste in a residential space must be sorted accordingly, or you will be fined. At the guest house I statyed at you had to separate food, vinyl, paper, and plastic.

Tech Observation: Google Maps is not supported in South Korea. The apps people prefer are called Naver and Kakao Map. These apps are exactly accurate, and when using public transportation, they display a fairly accurate estimated time of arrival (ETA).

Urbanization Observation:There are tons of cafes around Seoul, and they tend to open around noon and stay open until 10 pm.
