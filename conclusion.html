Overall Thoughts

Title description

South Korea is a country full of rich culture and history. The origins of this country are truly astonishing, and I am amazed at what the country is today. South Korea is honestly so beautiful and organized and very foreigner-friendly. I am not good at Korean by any means, but I used the little bit I did know and English, and I made it around just fine. This was such a good experience, especially as a solo female traveller. I would recommend it to anyone. Please do remember that there are different customs and cultural differences, but as long as you remai n respectful, you will be alright!
